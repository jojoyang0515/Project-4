Memorials in Washington D.C.

Washington, D.C. is a special city in all of American's heart. It is a city filled with actions, beautiful scenes, and historical stories. Moreover, Washington D.C. is a city of commemoration with more than a dozen memorials scattered all over the city. Not only are memorials popular locations where tourists go to when visiting D.C, memorials are also where we honor great individuals and groups who have dedicated themselves to protect our country and make it the beautiful land as it is today.
